Top outdoor picks

Short descriptions + why they’re worth doing.

Bridal Veil Falls

Quick walk, great photos, low effort. Easy “yes” for guests.

Best time: morning or golden hour

Provo River Trail

Paved trail for walking/biking. Scenic without feeling like a “hike.”

Move: start near the canyon entrance

Hike the Y

Iconic and steep. If you want a view fast, this delivers.

Bring: water + headlamp for sunset

Rock Canyon

Great views and climbing energy. Feels more “wild” than the Y.

Bonus: post-hike treat in Provo

South Fork Park

Picnic tables, river sounds, low commitment. Perfect reset.

Pair with: hot drink in winter

Sundance Village

Scenic walk + “mini trip” feeling. Great date-night option.

Heads up: can be pricier

Quick packing list

Keep it simple. This is “go outside,” not “expedition.”

Always

  • Water
  • Snacks
  • Layer (hoodie/windbreaker)
  • Charged phone

Nice to have

  • Headlamp (sunset hikes)
  • Blanket (picnic)
  • Micro first-aid
  • Camera (if you’re into it)

Winter

  • Warm socks
  • Hand warmers
  • Thermos drink
  • Traction (if icy)
